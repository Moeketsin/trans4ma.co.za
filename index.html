About

TRANS4MA cc PTY, LTD is a business consulting company that was established in 2015 by Sarah Khantshi. The founders of TRANS4MA are both qualified Industrial Engineers with formal degrees from some of the leading universities of South Africa. The idea behind the foundation of this company was the opportunity we identified within organisations. Where organisations assume to be productive because they are making profit and meeting targets. This is not the case at all, making profit is all good and well but what if we told you, by merely optimizing your processes we could maximise your profit?

TRANS4MA provides and specialises in business process re-engineering services.

We provide the following process solutions to SMMEs in Gauteng:

  • Process re-engineering- through work measurement and analysis we implement the best practice suited for the task to make sustainable improvements
  • Implementation of best practices namely:
    • 5S
    • Continuous improvement
    • Small Group Activities
    • Elimination of wasteful Activities
    • Goal Alignment
  • Implementation of Quality Management Systems mainly ISO 9001:2008
  • Conduct time and motion studies
